How to find your Shoe Size

Finding a shoe that fits perfectly can be difficult, but measuring your feet to find your size is great plae to Start. Knowing your footsize allows you to buy Fluff footware with confidence. Follow these steps to measure your feet and find that perfect fitting pair of shoes.
